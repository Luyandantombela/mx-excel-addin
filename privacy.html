Privacy Policy

MX Sheet Toolkit · Last updated: 27 June 2026

Overview

MX Sheet Toolkit is an Excel add-in developed by Luyanda Ntombela. This policy explains what data is collected when you use the add-in, how it is used, and what is stored on your device.

MX Sheet Toolkit does not collect, store, or transmit any of your spreadsheet data or file contents to any server.

Data we collect

MX Sheet Toolkit collects minimal anonymous usage data for the purpose of understanding how the add-in is used and improving it over time. Specifically:

  • A randomly generated anonymous user identifier (no name, email, or personal information)
  • Session events — which features were opened or used during a session
  • General usage frequency — how often the add-in is opened

This data contains no personally identifiable information. It cannot be used to identify you.

How data is sent

Anonymous usage events are sent to a Google Apps Script endpoint operated by the developer. This endpoint receives only the anonymous identifier and the event type — nothing about the content of your spreadsheet.

No data is shared with third-party advertising networks or sold to any party.

Local storage

MX Sheet Toolkit stores one item in your browser's local storage:

  • An anonymous user ID (mxt_uid) — a randomly generated string created on first use, used solely to group anonymous usage events from the same device

This identifier is stored locally on your device only. It is never linked to your name, email address, Microsoft account, or any other personal information.

You can clear this at any time by clearing your browser or Office add-in local storage.

Your spreadsheet data

MX Sheet Toolkit processes your spreadsheet data entirely within Excel on your device. No cell values, formulas, file names, or any spreadsheet content is ever transmitted outside of Excel.

All data cleaning, searching, editing, and comparison features operate locally within your Excel session.

Third-party services

MX Sheet Toolkit uses the following third-party service:

  • Google Apps Script — used to receive anonymous usage events. Google's privacy policy applies to this service and can be found at policies.google.com/privacy

Data retention

Anonymous usage data collected via Google Apps Script is retained for as long as necessary to understand usage patterns, and may be deleted at the developer's discretion. Because the data is anonymous and not linked to any individual, it cannot be retrieved or deleted on a per-user basis.

Children's privacy

MX Sheet Toolkit is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of MX Sheet Toolkit after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Frequently asked questions

How do I install MX Sheet Toolkit?
Visit our installation page for step-by-step instructions for Windows, Mac, and Excel on the web.
Is MX Sheet Toolkit free?
Yes, MX Sheet Toolkit is completely free to install and use.
Does the add-in send my spreadsheet data anywhere?
No. All standard cleaning, searching, editing, and comparison features work entirely within Excel on your device. Your data never leaves Excel unless you choose to use the optional AI cleaning feature, which is clearly labelled.
The add-in isn't showing in my Excel ribbon. What do I do?
Try closing Excel completely and reopening it. If it still doesn't appear, remove the add-in and reinstall it using the instructions on our installation page. Still stuck? Email us.
Which versions of Excel are supported?
MX Sheet Toolkit works on Excel 2013 or later on Windows, Excel 2016 or later on Mac, Excel on the web, and Microsoft 365 on all platforms.
I found a bug. How do I report it?
Email us at lntombela159@gmail.com with a description of what happened and what you were doing when it occurred. Screenshots are very helpful.
